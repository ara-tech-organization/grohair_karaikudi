Hair Treatments

Popular at Advanced GroHair & GloSkin Karaikudi

Our Most Popular Hair Treatments

At Advanced GroHair Karaikudi, we specialize in advanced hair restoration solutions designed to combat hair fall, thinning, and pattern baldness. Our treatments are scientifically backed and customized to help restore hair density, scalp health, and overall confidence.

Scalp Micropigmentation (SMP)

A non-surgical solution that uses advanced pigmenting to replicate natural hair follicles on the scalp. Ideal for those with thinning hair or shaved hairstyles looking for fuller-looking coverage.

Eyebrow Transplantation

Restore natural-looking eyebrows using your own hair. Perfect for sparse, over-plucked, or thinning brows, this procedure offers permanent, natural results.

StemX27

An innovative hair rejuvenation treatment that uses stem cell-derived growth factors to stimulate dormant hair follicles and improve scalp vitality.

Mesotherapy

A minimally invasive treatment where nutrient-rich serums are injected directly into the scalp to strengthen roots, reduce hair fall, and encourage regrowth.

Oxygen Laser Therapy

A gentle, revitalizing laser technique combined with oxygen infusion to nourish the scalp, promote circulation, and enhance hair health.
